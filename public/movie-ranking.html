The Best Movies According to KayCee

My Top three of all-time

X-MEN

The X-Men, created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby in 1963, are a team of mutants with extraordinary abilities. Led by Professor X, they fight for peace and equality in a world that fears them. Key characters include Cyclops, Wolverine, and Storm. The franchise explores themes of prejudice and identity, with recurring villains like Magneto and Apocalypse.

Alien; Romulus

Alien: Romulus is a comic series set in the Alien universe, introducing a new setting on the planet Romulus. Survivors must navigate a dangerous environment infested with Xenomorphs. The story adds depth to the Alien mythos, focusing on survival and the terror of encountering the deadly creatures.
